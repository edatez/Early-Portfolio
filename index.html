About Me


Ever since I was young, I was deeply influenced by cultures and travelling. I am passionate about design. I am curious and all the time hungry about technology. I am a Seattle based UX Designer with a masters in industrial design. I strongly believe that designs should be user centered and intuitive as well as functional, customizable even personalized but overall of course sustainable, and environment friendly.
